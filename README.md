\# 《情境教学视域下的语文大单元教学策略研究——以〈师说〉〈拿来主义〉〈谏逐客书〉为例》访谈数据》



\## 论文摘要



在新课标核心素养导向下，情境教学与大单元教学的深度融合是落实语文学习任务群的关键路径，许多学者及一线教师在此需求下为二者理论的实践转化作出了尝试，然此类试验仍存在情境创设形式化、大单元整体性认识不足等现实困境。本研究旨在根据重庆县域中学的现实状况构建与之适配的情境教学指导下的语文大单元教学策略体系，采用文献研究法，在系统梳理了“情境教学”“大单元教学”及“学习任务群”的概念特征与内在关联后，以此为理论基础，基于CIPP模型，为“情境教学视域下语文大单元教学”这一核心理念面向重庆县域中学教师设计了半结构化访谈，发掘出其“自发而不自觉”的实践现状及课时紧张、评价体系滞后、资源匮乏等结构性困境，并据此提出相应设计应遵循情境真实性、任务链整合性、知识综合性、活动实践性的设计原则，构建了涵盖教学目标确定、教学内容统整、教学流程组织、教学评价设计等具体的实施策略。在以上原则与策略的基础上，本研究围绕《师说》《拿来主义》《谏逐客书》三篇文章设计了一份教学方案样例。针对样例的二次访谈验证表明，以上策略能有效指导县域高中开展情境教学指导的语文大单元教学设计，为破解实践难题、推动素养导向的教学转型提供了相应方案。



\## 关键词：情境教学；大单元教学；教学策略



\## Abstract



Under the guidance of core competencies stipulated in the new curriculum standards, the deep integration of situated learning and large-unit teaching constitutes a pivotal pathway for implementing Chinese language learning task groups. Although scholars and frontline teachers have tried to translate these theories into practice under such demands, these experiments still face practical troubles including formalistic contextual creation and insufficient holistic understanding of large-unit teaching. This study Aims to construct a compatible strategic system for large-unit Chinese language teaching guided by contextualized teaching according to the realistic conditions of county high schools in Chongqing. It also employs the literature research method. After systematically analyzing the conceptual features and intrinsic correlations among "situated learning", "large-unit teaching" and "learning task groups" as the theoretical foundation, and based on the CIPP evaluation model, semi-structured interviews were designed for teachers in county high schools in Chongqing regarding the core concept of "large-unit Chinese language teaching from the perspective of situated learning." The investigation revealed a practical status characterized as "spontaneous yet unconscious," alongside structural dilemmas including tight class schedules, lagging evaluation systems, and resource scarcity. Taking these exposed predicaments as entry points, the study proposes design principles encompassing the authenticity of context, integration of task chains, comprehensiveness of knowledge, and practicality of activities. It further constructs specific implementation strategies covering instructional objective determination, teaching content integration, teaching process organization, and instructional evaluation design. Building upon these principles and strategies, a sample teaching plan was designed around three texts called Shi Shuo, Na Lai Zhu Yi and Jian Zhu Ke Shu. Validation through follow-up interviews demonstrated that these strategies can effectively guide county high schools in designing large-unit Chinese language teaching under situated learning, providing corresponding solutions for resolving practical difficulties and promoting literacy-oriented instructional transformation.
